ABOUT

Established in 2004 by director, Masaki Yukioka, Velvet Lounge features unique accessories for modern day men. Jewelry has been a strong piece of wearable art that has been passed on from the past, which continues to evolve today. By fusing the past ideals with present day themes, we present a new classic line to mens fashion and jewelry. Velvet Lounge continues to seek new ideas and designs to create a new start to fashion.

COLLECTION

"Nothing is invented, for it's written in nature first."

Antoni Gaudi

Our new collection "Gaudi" features the talented ideals of Antoni Gaudi. We mixed his beautiful sense of architectural structure and color with Velvet Lounge's unique filters. The accessory designs are inspired by different architectural structures and motifs by Gaudi, as well as fusing the beauty of nature. The style, color, and material are strictly concept-based, featuring Antoni Gaudi from different perspectives. Though the accessories are on a rather smaller scale, they have a strong presence in itself to fit your taste.
